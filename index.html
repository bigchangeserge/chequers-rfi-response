BigChange is proudly UK based tech built for businesses that carry out high volumes of daily jobbing work, offering powerful tools to streamline operations and enhance customer service. Its smart scheduling and bulk route optimisation capabilities ensure the right resource is sent to the right job at the right time—taking into account location, traffic, skills, and availability. With real-time proof of works, time-stamped job updates, and automatic customer alerts, BigChange helps businesses deliver a world-class customer journey.

Core Markets:

  • Grounds Maintenance
  • Waste & Drainage
  • Soft & Hard Services

AroFlo is a cloud-based job and field service management platform designed specifically for smaller Australian trade and service businesses, with headquarters in Australia. It offers end-to-end capabilities—from quoting, scheduling, and invoicing to asset tracking, compliance forms, and GPS monitoring—enabling tradespeople to manage their entire job lifecycle seamlessly.

Core Markets:

  • Asset Industries
  • Facilities Management
  • Project Management

ClockShark is a cloud-based timesheet software designed for small field service businesses, particularly in Australia and the U.S. It enables mobile teams to easily clock in and out using GPS-enabled mobile app, ensuring accurate time tracking with geofencing and location-based verification. With features like crew clock-in, real-time visibility of who’s working, and seamless accountancy integrations. It simplifies timesheet collection, reduces admin overhead.

Core Markets:

  • Construction
  • Cleaning Services
  • Staffing

Simpro is an Australian based field service management platform designed primarily for project-based businesses, particularly in industries like HVAC, electrical, and plumbing. It excels at managing long-term projects, complex quoting, and asset management—tracking equipment such as boilers and air conditioning units throughout their lifecycle. Its scheduling capabilities are relatively basic and better suited to planned works than reactive or high-frequency jobbing environments. Simpro isn't built for businesses that rely on fast-paced, high-volume jobbing.

BigChange's Story & Timeline

From Startup to Industry Leader - A Journey of Innovation & Growth

Founded by Martin Port

2013

Martin Port, a serial entrepreneur from Leeds, founded BigChange with the goal of revolutionising the field service management industry, bringing together job management, CRM and vehicle tracking in one native platform.

Rapid Growth

2013-2020

BigChange experienced explosive growth - scaling from launch to over $30 million in annual recurring revenue by 2020, and acquiring around 2000 customers by that point.

GHP Investment

2021

In February 2021, BigChange received a £75 million growth investment from Boston-based private equity firm Great Hill Partners, valuing the business at around £100 million. At the time, BigChange had a team of 150 employees driving its rapid growth and innovation.

K1 Partners Investment

2024

In November 2024, BigChange was acquired by Australian field service management provider Simpro Group, backed by K1 Investment Management—marking a major milestone in its growth journey. As part of the acquisition, BigChange joined a powerful portfolio of field service software solutions, including AroFlo, ClockShark, and Simpro Software.
